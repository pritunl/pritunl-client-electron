Installation

You will be guided through the steps necessary to install this software. This installer includes the Pritunl Client app and helper service required for creating connections.

Uninstallation

All files and services may be uninstalled by first closing the client app and dragging the Pritunl app from your applications to the trash. A restart may be required to finish the removal process. This will also remove any vpn profiles. If you wish to keep the vpn profiles, they will need to be copied from the Application Support directory.
